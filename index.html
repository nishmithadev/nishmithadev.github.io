SIGNAL: MARKET → DECISION

Risk-Aware Portfolio Optimization

An autonomous trading agent that manages a multi-stock portfolio using deep reinforcement learning. It reads price history, technical indicators, and financial-news sentiment, then learns — through a Deep Q-Network trained on the Bellman equation — when to buy, sell, or hold. Instead of chasing raw profit, it optimizes for the Sharpe ratio, weighing return against risk the way a real portfolio manager would.
